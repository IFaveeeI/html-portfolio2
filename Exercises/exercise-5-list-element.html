Sander's Cinnamon Roll Recipe Ingredients For the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For FILLING:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  2. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  4. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
  5. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
